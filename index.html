Winter ❄️

  • Winter is a chilly season.
  • People wear warm clothes.
  • Snowfall may occur in some regions.
  • Days are shorter and nights are longer.

Spring 🌸

  • Spring is a season of blooming flowers.
  • The weather becomes warmer.
  • Animals come out of hibernation.
  • Trees grow new green leaves.
